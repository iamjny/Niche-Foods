Green Inferno Smoothie


This recipe is an instant energy booster infused with vitamin and hydration.

Blend 2 peeled kiwis, 1 banana, 1 cup of baby spinach, 1 celery, 1/4 ginger root 1/2 cup of water, juice or plant-based milk to your taste. Add 10 ice cubes for an extra refreshing drink.

Quick recipes for lazy days


100% vegan and easy-to-make tofu bowl

Bake super firm pressed tofu in Tahini sauce for about 20 minutes.
We recommend using kale and quinoa as base, but you are free to use whatever you have at home.
Add edamame, red cabbage, carrots, broccoli, cucumber, any kind of squash and mix it together. Tofu can be replaced with seitan for wheat / gluten options or even mushrooms.
